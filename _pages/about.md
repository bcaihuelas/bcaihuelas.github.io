I am a political scientist and postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Social Sciences at the Universidad Carlos III de Madrid (UC3M), where I work on the Horizon Europe project EXPRESS2: Specify and Protect the EU Social Contract. I hold a PhD in Social Sciences from UC3M (2025).
My research lies at the intersection of political behavior, public opinion, and political inequality in contemporary European democracies. I am particularly interested in how socio-economic position and social status shape political preferences and participation, and in the dynamics of political trust in the European Union.

I combine quantitative and comparative methods, working with large cross-national survey data, longitudinal designs, and experimental approaches. 

During my doctoral and postdoctoral work I have held visiting positions at the Humboldt University of Berlin, the Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona, the Universitat de Barcelona, and the European University Institute.
